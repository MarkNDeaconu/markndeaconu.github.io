### From the Park to the Feed

After seeing the genuine joy her portraits sparked in people, I decided to document these interactions on video. As we began posting them to social media, the reaction was instant. We garnered thousands of views within days, which inspired us to return to the park as often as possible. 

The momentum was unstoppable. After hitting **millions of total views**, our small park project evolved into something much bigger. We began receiving invitations from charities asking if we could help raise funds at their events.
